Course Structure

Objectives

  • Develop a global understanding of the various data sources available for studying social phenomena.

  • Develop the autonomy required to collect, manage, and quantitatively analyze databases, and integrate them into a research project.

  • Demonstrate an understanding of the issues related to mobilizing and using large databases in the social sciences.
